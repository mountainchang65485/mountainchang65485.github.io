Hi! My name is Mountain Chang!

I'm a designer based in Taipei, Taiwan. I graduated from National Taiwan University of Arts, majored in fine arts and mainly focus on photography, mix media installations and graphic design.
Most of my works are inspired from life experience. Especially on the memory of my family, self-awareness, people and land, life and death. I transfer my life experience into the key element of my works.
Recently, I'm trying to explore my horizon of all kinds , which makes me I keep improving my design and coding ability.
As Walter Gropius (1883-1969) once advocated, we should breaking the barrier between fine arts and applied arts.This is what I pursued for the all time.
